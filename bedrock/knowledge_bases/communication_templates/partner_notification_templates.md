## 4. School Superintendent Notification

**Subject:** Health Advisory: [DISEASE] — Information for [SCHOOL_DISTRICT]

---

Dear Superintendent [SUPERINTENDENT_NAME],

I'm writing to inform you of a [DISEASE] preparation advisory for [COUNTY_NAME]. This is a COURTESY notification — no school action is required at this time.

**Summary:**
The [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department is monitoring [DISEASE] signals in our region. Based on patterns in [METRO_NAME], we expect increased illness in our community in approximately [LAG_WEEKS] weeks.

**Current Status:** Advisory (preparation phase)
**School Impact Now:** None — schools operate normally
**Possible Future Impact:** Increased absenteeism expected in [TIMEFRAME]

**What We're Asking Schools to Do NOW:**
1. Monitor daily attendance for unusual patterns
2. Reinforce handwashing and respiratory hygiene with students
3. Ensure hand sanitizer dispensers are stocked
4. Review sick-child policies with parents (fever = stay home)
5. Designate a school point of contact for health department communication

**We Will Notify You IMMEDIATELY If:**
- We recommend any change to school operations
- Absenteeism thresholds are reached that warrant discussion
- School closure becomes a consideration

**School Closure:**
School closure will ONLY be recommended if:
- Absenteeism exceeds 10% school-wide from illness
- CDC classifies severity as "High" or "Very High" with pediatric impact
- Mutual agreement between health department and school administration

Decision will be made COLLABORATIVELY — not unilaterally.

**Next Communication:** [DATE] (or sooner if situation changes)

Please don't hesitate to reach out with questions. This is what early warning is for — so we have time to plan together.

[HEALTH_OFFICER_NAME], [CREDENTIALS]
[TITLE], [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department

## 5. Long-Term Care Facility Alert

**Subject:** LTCF Advisory: [DISEASE] Preparation — [SEVERITY] | [COUNTY_NAME] Health Dept

---

Dear [FACILITY_ADMINISTRATOR],

**Long-Term Care Facility [DISEASE] Preparation Advisory**

The [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department is issuing this advisory to all licensed long-term care facilities in our jurisdiction. Increased [DISEASE] activity is anticipated within [LAG_WEEKS] weeks.

**Why This Matters for Your Facility:**
Your residents are among the highest-risk populations for severe [DISEASE] outcomes. Early preparation can significantly reduce transmission and severity within your facility.

**IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (implement within 1 week):**

1. **Vaccination:**
   - Verify all eligible residents are vaccinated for [DISEASE]
   - Offer vaccination to any unvaccinated staff
   - Document vaccination rates (target: >90% residents, >75% staff)

2. **Infection Control:**
   - Review and post respiratory illness protocols
   - Ensure adequate PPE supply ([DURATION]-week stock: masks, gowns, gloves)
   - Establish respiratory illness screening at all entry points
   - Designate isolation area for symptomatic residents

3. **Surveillance:**
   - Implement twice-daily symptom monitoring (temperature + respiratory symptoms)
   - Establish reporting threshold: notify health dept if ≥2 respiratory illness cases in 72 hours
   - Reporting line: [HEALTH_DEPT_PHONE]

4. **Visitation:**
   - Consider enhanced visitor screening (symptom check at entry)
   - Post signage asking symptomatic visitors not to enter
   - Provide masks to all visitors during elevated activity period

5. **Antiviral Access:**
   - Verify facility pharmacy can supply oseltamivir within 24 hours of symptom onset
   - For COVID-19: confirm Paxlovid access pathway (within 5 days of symptoms)
   - Establish standing orders for antiviral initiation (pending physician confirmation)

**OUTBREAK RESPONSE (if ≥2 cases in 72 hours):**
- Notify [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department IMMEDIATELY: [PHONE]
- Implement droplet precautions for ALL residents on affected unit
- Restrict new admissions to affected unit
- Restrict staff movement between units
- Collect specimens for laboratory confirmation

**Resources:**
- [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department: [PHONE]
- State LTCF Infection Preventionist: [STATE_IP_PHONE]
- CDC LTCF Guidance: https://www.cdc.gov/longtermcare/

[HEALTH_OFFICER_NAME], [CREDENTIALS]
[TITLE], [COUNTY_NAME] Health Department
